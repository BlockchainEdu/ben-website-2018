Our History

The Blockchain Education network was founded in February of 2014. Initially, we were named the College Cryptocurrency Network. We started as an effort to connect students across campuses. Few knew or cared about cryptocurrency at the time - by ourselves in our dorm rooms, we felt alone but together, we felt like we were in on the biggest secret of our generation. The Blockchain Education Network was an effort to unite early adopter students to create something much bigger than ourselves.
